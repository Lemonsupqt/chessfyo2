## ğŸ® How to Play

### Local Game (Same Device)
1. Open the game
2. Click "Begin the Duel"
3. Take turns moving pieces
4. White player starts

### vs AI (Computer Opponent)
1. Click "Face the Algorithm"
2. You play as White
3. AI plays as Black
4. Choose difficulty in sidebar

### Online Multiplayer (With Friends)
1. Click "Enter the Network"

**If you're hosting:**
- Enter your name
- Click "Create Room"
- Share the 6-letter code with your friend

**If you're joining:**
- Enter your name
- Enter your friend's room code
- Click "Join the Battle"

Both players must be on the game at the same URL!
