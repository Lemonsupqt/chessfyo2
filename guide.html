Online Match

Play with friends anywhere in the world using peer-to-peer connections:

  • Host: Click "Create Game" and share the link with your friend
  • Join: Enter the Game ID or click the shared link
  • No account or server needed - completely free!

Basic Controls

  • Make a Move: Click a piece to select it, then click a destination square
  • Legal Moves: Available moves are highlighted when you select a piece
  • Capture: Moves that capture opponent pieces show a red circle
  • Deselect: Click the selected piece again or click elsewhere

Special Moves

  • Castling: Move your king two squares toward a rook (if legal)
  • En Passant: Automatically available when legal
  • Pawn Promotion: Automatically promotes to Queen (reaching the end)

üåê Online Play Tips

Connection Tips

  • Both players need a stable internet connection
  • The connection is direct peer-to-peer (no server involved)
  • If connection fails, try creating a new game
  • Works best on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

Sharing Your Game

  • Copy the game link and send it via messaging app, email, or social media
  • Or share just the Game ID for your friend to enter manually
  • The creator (host) always plays as White
  • The joiner always plays as Black

üí° Strategy Tips

Opening Principles

  • Control the center (e4, d4, e5, d5)
  • Develop your knights and bishops early
  • Castle early to protect your king
  • Don't move the same piece twice in the opening

General Strategy

  • Protect your pieces and king
  • Look for tactical opportunities (forks, pins, skewers)
  • Think about your opponent's threats before making a move
  • In the endgame, activate your king

Against Stockfish

  • Start with lower difficulties and work your way up
  • Focus on solid, principled play rather than tricks
  • Study your games - see where you went wrong
  • Don't get discouraged - even Novice level can be challenging!
